## Detailed Feature Sections (alternating left/right layout)

---

### Feature 1 — Arabic Alphabet
**Section Headline:** *Start from Alif. Build from the ground up.*

**Description:**
The Arabic Alphabet module teaches you all 28 letters with their isolated, initial, medial, and final forms. You learn the harakat (fatha, kasra, damma, sukoon, tanween) with audio and visual cues. Lessons are bite-sized, interactive, and progressively unlock.

**What you'll learn:**
- All 28 Arabic letters and their shapes
- Vowel marks (harakat) — short and long vowels
- Sun and moon letters (shamsi & qamari)
- Letter joining and word formation basics

**Screenshot placeholder:** Tap-to-reveal letter card with audio button

---

### Feature 2 — Surah Memorisation
**Section Headline:** *Never forget a Surah again.*

**Description:**
Our Surah module uses a proven 3-stage system: Listen → Repeat → Recall. Each Surah is broken into short segments. You hear a certified Qari, repeat with the mic, then recall from memory. Revision levels refresh verses at spaced intervals so they stay locked in.

**Surah Library (sample):**
- Juz Amma: Al-Fatiha, Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, An-Nas, Al-Kawthar, Ad-Dhuha *(and 30+ more)*
- Premium: Full 114 Surah library

---

### Feature 3 — AI Recitation Feedback
**Section Headline:** *Your personal tajweed coach. Available 24/7.*

**Description:**
Using advanced speech recognition (powered by OpenAI Whisper), Ustad listens to your recitation and gives you real-time feedback. It detects common tajweed errors — elongation (madd), ghunna, and letter confusion — and shows you exactly what to fix.

**How it works:**
1. Tap the microphone icon
2. Recite the highlighted ayah
3. Ustad scores your accuracy (0–100%)
4. View colour-coded feedback word by word
5. Re-attempt until perfected

---

### Feature 4 — Duas & Supplications
**Section Headline:** *Make every moment an act of worship.*

**Description:**
Daily life is full of sunnah moments — waking up, eating, travelling, sleeping. Ustad teaches you the authentic Duas for each occasion with Arabic text, transliteration, translation, and audio. Earn XP every time you practice.

**Dua Categories:**
- Morning & Evening Adhkar
- Before & After Meals
- Entering & Leaving Home
- Dua before Sleep
- Protection Duas
- Travel Duas

---

### Feature 5 — Pronunciation Coach
**Section Headline:** *Say it like the Sahaba.*

**Description:**
Arabic has sounds that don't exist in English or Urdu. The Pronunciation Coach breaks down Makharij Al-Huruf (articulation points) using visual throat/mouth diagrams and slow-motion audio. Master ع، ح، خ، غ، ق and other challenging letters.

---

### Feature 6 — Surah Stories *(Coming Soon)*
**Section Headline:** *Connect with Quran through story.*

**Description:**
Each Surah has a context, a reason for revelation, and a message. Our Surah Stories module uses illustrated characters and narrative to bring the meaning of each Surah to life — making memorisation meaningful, not just mechanical.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

**Step 1 — Download & Sign Up (2 min)**
> Download Ustad from the App Store or Google Play. Create a free account with your email or continue as a guest. No payment needed.

**Step 2 — Take the Placement Quiz (3 min)**
> Answer 5 quick questions: Can you read Arabic? Which Surahs do you know? What's your daily goal? Ustad builds your personalised learning path instantly.

**Step 3 — Complete Your First Lesson (5–10 min)**
> Your first lesson is carefully designed to give you a quick win. You'll learn 3 Arabic letters, earn your first XP badge, and unlock your 7-day challenge.

**Step 4 — Build Your Streak**
> Set a daily reminder. Even 5 minutes counts. Ustad tracks your streak and sends gentle reminders so you never break the habit.

**Step 5 — Level Up & Revise**
> As you progress through levels, Ustad automatically schedules revision sessions for content you've already learned — so you never forget what you've memorised.

## Learning Science Section

**Headline:** *The Science Behind Ustad*

**Sub-headline:** *We didn't invent gamified learning — we perfected it for Quran.*

**Three Science Cards:**

| Card | Concept | Explanation |
|---|---|---|
| 🧠 | **Spaced Repetition** | Content you've learned reappears at optimal intervals — when your memory is about to fade. This is the most effective memorisation technique known to science. |
| 🎮 | **Variable Reward** | Earning XP, maintaining streaks, and unlocking badges triggers dopamine — the same mechanism behind great games. We channel that into Quran learning. |
| 🔁 | **Active Recall** | Instead of just reading, Ustad makes you produce Arabic from memory. This "desirable difficulty" dramatically improves long-term retention. |
